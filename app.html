How to Use StreetSmart Cincinnati

Report a Pothole

Help improve Cincinnati's roads by reporting potholes you encounter:

  • Click anywhere on the map to select the pothole location
  • Fill in your name (optional) and description
  • Select severity: Low, Medium, or High
  • Click "Submit Repair Request" to send your report

Explore Different Data

Use the layer control (top right of map) to view different types of information:

  • Official Potholes: City-reported open pothole cases
  • User Requests: Community-submitted reports
  • Street View: Available Mapillary street imagery
  • AI Detected: Machine learning identified road damage

AI-Powered Detection

Our system uses advanced AI to automatically identify potholes:

  • Red with gold border: High confidence detections (80%+)
  • Orange: Medium confidence detections (60-80%)
  • Yellow: Lower confidence detections (40-60%)
  • Click any AI marker to view the street image where damage was detected

Street View Integration

View actual street-level imagery of reported locations:

  • Click green camera markers to view available street imagery
  • Real-time AI analysis runs on street view images
  • Navigate through Mapillary imagery to see road conditions
  • Use the back button to return to the main map

Community Engagement

Support important community reports by voting:

  • Click upvote buttons on user-submitted reports
  • Higher-voted reports get more visibility
  • Help prioritize the most critical infrastructure issues
  • View upvote counts in both the map popups and sidebar list
